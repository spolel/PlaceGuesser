PLACE GUESSER

HOW TO PLAY


You get images from a random place on earth and you have to guess where it is.

You have 5 rounds. Place you guess on the map.
The maximum points you can reach are 5000. You gan get a maximum of 1000 points per round depending on how close you were.

At the end of the game you will be shown a summary showing all your guesses on the map.
